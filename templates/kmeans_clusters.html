KMeans Clustering

Using KMeans clustering to group Netflix titles into clusters based on similar features, such as genres. It's a method to partition a dataset into groups that share similar characteristics.

Understanding the Chart:

Genre Direction 1: This represents a combination of genres that together explain the most variation in the movies and series in the dataset. It might be thought of as a spectrum along which titles are similar or different based on a particular mix of genres.

Genre Direction 2: This is another combination of genres, orthogonal to the first, that captures the second most variation. It provides another way to differentiate titles based on their genres.
